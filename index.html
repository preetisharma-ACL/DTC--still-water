About the Developer – MAX Group

MAX Group is a trusted real estate brand known for delivering premium luxury developments with strong investment potential. The developer has shown a proven track record of price appreciation, making its projects highly desirable in the Noida market. About 2 years ago, MAX launched a project in Sector 128, Noida at ₹17,000/sq.ft, and today its resale value has reached ₹26,000/sq.ft, clearly highlighting the brand’s consistent growth and demand. Continuing this legacy, MAX Sector 105 is a high-end residential project spread across 10.34 acres with 3.5 acres of landscaped green spaces, offering low-density living with only 4 apartments per floor across 37 floors in 2 iconic towers. With world-class amenities, premium infrastructure, and future-ready planning, MAX Sector 105 stands out as an elevated luxury address designed for modern urban living and long-term appreciation.
